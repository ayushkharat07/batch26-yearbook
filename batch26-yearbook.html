Finding Our Rhythm

Second year hit different. We found our tribes, our haunts, our routines. Lab sessions that stretched past midnight, chai breaks that turned into two-hour conversations. We hosted our first major events and realized what we were capable of.

A Goodbye Worth Remembering

Fiesta, Chaos & Glory

The annual fest was ours to run. Months of planning condensed into three days of electric energy. We danced, performed, competed, and collapsed — and then laughed about it for weeks. This was the year we truly became legends of the campus.

The Last Chapter

Final exams, last lectures, and the bittersweet air of endings. We sat in classrooms that felt smaller now, walked corridors we could navigate blindfolded. And then came the final farewell — our own. It was everything we hoped it would be.
